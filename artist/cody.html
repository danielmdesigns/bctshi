Cody Zeek

Cody has been a full time tattooer since 2008. Originally apprenticing under Mike Ledger in a private studio in Honolulu, he went on to tattoo in Waikiki for five years before relocating to Portland Oregon. There he worked at Scapegoat Tattoo and settled into the style of eerie thin-line black work he is known for. Cody returned home to Honolulu in 2016 and joined Black Cat Tattoo.
