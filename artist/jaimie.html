Jaimie Coloma

Jaimie Coloma was born and raised in Hilo, on the Big Island of Hawaii. She now lives and works full time on Oahu, but still goes back to Hilo to work often. Art and creativity has always been a part of her life, but she never knew how much passion she had for it, until she started tattooing in 2009.

She enjoys doing many styles of tattooing but super enjoys doing neo-traditional, color fades, small details, all seeing eyes, and black and gray. She is continuously amazed by all her talented coworkers around her, and loves learning new tricks of the trade.
